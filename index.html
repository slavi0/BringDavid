Meet David

David is a bright and curious child with autism, but despite his eagerness to learn, he has faced rejection from every school his parents have approached. Each institution, citing a lack of resources or specialized support, turned him away, leaving his family feeling frustrated and heartbroken. While other children his age make friends and explore new subjects in the classroom, David is left without a place to belong. His parents refuse to give up, determined to find an environment where he will be accepted, supported, and given the opportunity to thrive like any other child.
